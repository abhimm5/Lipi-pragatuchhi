Lipi pragatuchhi is designed to revive old Odia script, which had many unique looking feature than the current Odia script. Although most Odia fonts follow single design principle but this follows both cicular and rounded rectangular shape.

This is designed by looking old script and reseaching them separately about the style. This font resembles them with unique contemporary touch.

To contribute, see github.com/abhimm5/Lipipra-font.
